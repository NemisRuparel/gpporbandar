Clean & Green Campus



Our college's campus offers a beautiful, serene environment, as seen in this peaceful walkway lined with towering trees and vibrant greenery. The shaded paths provide a cool, refreshing ambiance, perfect for leisurely walks and moments of reflection. The well-preserved natural surroundings not only enhance the aesthetic appeal but also reflect our commitment to maintaining an eco-friendly atmosphere. The combination of nature and cleanliness creates a welcoming space that encourages both relaxation and inspiration for students, staff, and visitors alike.


NCC unit



Our college is proud to host a dedicated NCC unit, fostering discipline, leadership, and a spirit of national service among students. The building, surrounded by lush greenery, serves as a hub for training and activities, instilling values of unity and self-reliance. The presence of this NCC unit encourages students to actively participate in drills, camps, and community services, preparing them for future roles in the defense forces or civil services. It stands as a testament to our commitment to developing well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.


Resource utilization centre



The library features a diverse collection of books, journals, and digital materials across various subjects, catering to the needs of all students. With comfortable study areas and access to computers, it serves as an ideal environment for research and self-study. The library also hosts regular events and workshops, encouraging students to enhance their learning experience and make the most of the resources available to them.


RTO Services



The RTO Office at Government Polytechnic, Porbandar, offers a range of services related to vehicle registration, driving licenses, and transport documentation. Situated within the campus, this facility provides easy access to important transportation services for students, staff, and the local community. The office plays a key role in streamlining the administrative processes for vehicle-related formalities, making it a convenient hub for all transport-related needs.
