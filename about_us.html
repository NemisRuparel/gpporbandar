About Our Institute



Government Polytechnic, Porbandar was established in 1960 and is situated in Porbandar, Gujarat. It is an
esteemed institution affiliated with Gujarat Technological University, Ahmedabad, and is approved by the All
India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The college's expansive campus covers 35 acres, providing a
conducive environment for technical education.

The polytechnic offers a variety of diploma programs in engineering disciplines, including Electrical Engineering,
Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering and Computer Engineering. These programs are
designed to provide students with a robust foundation in their respective fields, preparing them for successful
careers.

Facilities at Government Polytechnic, Porbandar include well-equipped hostels for both boys and girls, a
comprehensive library with an extensive collection of books and journals, and modern IT infrastructure with advanced
computer labs. The college also boasts state-of-the-art laboratories to facilitate practical learning and has
transport facilities for the convenience of students. Additionally, students have access to sports facilities, which
contribute to their overall development.

Admissions to Government Polytechnic, Porbandar are based on merit. Prospective students can find detailed
information about the application process, deadlines, and fee structures on the college's official website. The
placement cell at the institution plays a crucial role in helping students secure job placements. It organizes
recruitment drives with reputed companies, conducts seminars and mock tests, and offers career counseling to assist
students in navigating the job market.
